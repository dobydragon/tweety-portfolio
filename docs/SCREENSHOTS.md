## In the site
The **Screens** section renders these in a filterable gallery
(All · Onboarding & Auth · Feed · Chat · Profile & People · System) with a
keyboard-navigable lightbox (← → to move, Esc to close). Images are lazy-loaded
and shown without cropping distortion.
